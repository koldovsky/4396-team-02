Racha Yai

Ideal for beginners. Experienced divers come here only to get in shape after long breaks between dives. The main advantages of Koh Racha Yai are: 20 km from Phuket, a shallow sandy bottom, clear calm water and good visibility throughout the year.

Anemone Reef

A limestone rock rising from a depth of 30 meters. Its eastern slope is a gentle descent to the sandy bottom, while the western side is a sheer wall. The abundance of anemones and nutrient-rich water attract incredible amounts of tropical fish to Anemone Reef.

King Cruiser

King Cruiser is a large car ferry that sank in 1997 after colliding with the underwater part of Anemone Reef. It has become an artificial coral reef. Only certified divers can dive here. Ideal for fans of wreck diving and underwater mysteries.
