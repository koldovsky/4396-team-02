Our Equipment

Speedboat RIB DIVERSAINT

The RIB DIVERSAINT high-speed boat is equipped with two Yamaha engines, which allows you to get to any dive sites three times faster than large diving ships. It is used for one-day diving in the Phuket water area.

DIVERACE CLASS E

Launched in 2015, the MV DiveRACE Class E safari ship has a length of 34.5 m and a width of 7.5 m. The dive deck is spacious enough for any diver, both for amateurs and techies. 6 twin cabins are located on the main deck.
