PADI Divemaster Course

The PADI Divemaster course is the first professional level. The course consists of an extensive theoretical part, and also includes assistance to the instructor in the pool and in open water. To join the course, you need to have a PADI AOWD or equivalent certificate . Also you should have a PADI Rescue Diver certificate or equivalent, be at least 18 years old and have a good physical shape. The duration of this course depends on your previous experience as by the end of the course, you need to have at least 60 registered dives in open water.
Quantity of dives: no less that 60
Approximate Duration: 15 days

Experienced instructors: learn to be a proficient scuba diver from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.
