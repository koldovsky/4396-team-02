PADI Open Water Course

A course for beginners with an international certificate. As part of the course, students are introduced to diving equipment, the basics of scuba diving, and various techniques. Upon successful completion of the course, a PADI Open Water Diver certificate is issued, which is recognized worldwide. To get it, you need to attend theoretical classes, make 1-2 dives in the pool with an instructor and 4 dives in open water (at sea), correctly complete the necessary actions and pass the test exam.
Quantity of dives: 4+pool
Approximate Duration: 3-4 days

Minimum Age: 10 years old
Experienced instructors: Learn the basics of scuba diving from a friendly and highly qualified team of trainers.
Practice: The course includes theoretical lessons and mandatory exercises in the pool, plus 3-4 dives in the sea.
Diving from childhood: the course is available for kids from 10 y.o. For students aged 10 to 12 years, the maximum diving depth is 12m.
